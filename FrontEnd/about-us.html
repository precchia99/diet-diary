Spring 2024 C&I Capstone

As a project for this course, our team is working on creating a website that enhances a physical diet diary. Allowing users to log, track, and review their diet goals and habits, Diet Diary seeks to make eating, easier. Diet is an essential part of life. Tracking eating habits provides one with several benefits, including:
  • Raising awareness about your eating habits
  • To achieve goals
  • Understand sensitivities/deficiencies
